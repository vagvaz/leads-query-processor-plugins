The Adidas Adizero Adios Boost Racing Trainer provides endless energy for the long run. This breakthrough marathon shoe is powered by boost foam and features a supportive mesh upper for stability while in motion.

Top Features of the Adidas Adizero Adios Boost Shoes - SS14

Heel-Toe Offset: 10mm
Continental rubber outsole for grip
Boost foam for comfort with responsiveness
Molded EVA liner for comfort and fit
Coolever mesh to manage heat and sweat
Tirennina Suede environmentally responsible upper

Product Data

Mens: Yes
Shoe Weight: 220g (UK 8.5)

Boost foam:
Offers the best energy return, due to thousands of energy capsules storing and unleashing energy with every step
Coolever:
Mesh and lining has a clover shaped yarn to manage heat and sweat reducing discomfort and increasing performance
Continental rubber:
Continental outsole rubber supplies superior grip in all underfoot conditions
Extended Torsion System
Provides great forefoot energy return and promotes natural torsion in the midfoot

Upper: Environmentally responsible, solvent free synthetic Tirennina suede
Inlay: Molded responsive EVA sockliner for anatomical fit and great step-in comfort

About the Adidas brand

Adidas is one of the best known brand-names in the world of sports and leisure products. The British Cycling team achieved many honors at the 2008 Olympics in the Adidas Olympic Skinsuits. The high quality 3 stripe branded jerseys, shorts and socks are made all over the world for road and off-road cyclists. 365 days a year of cycling comfort are assured with the Adidas cycling range of Climaproof, Climawarm and Climacool garments.
